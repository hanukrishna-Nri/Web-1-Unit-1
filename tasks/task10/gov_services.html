Citizen Services
Access all government services in one place. Many services are available fully online.

🪪 Identity & Civil Registration
→ Aadhaar Enrolment / Update Update address, name, date of birth or mobile number on Aadhaar Apply Online  Online 
→ PAN Card Application New PAN, correction, e-PAN download. Required for tax filings and financial transactions Apply Online  Online 
→ Passport Application Fresh passport, renewal, minor passport. Book appointment at Passport Seva Kendra Apply Online  Online 
→ Voter ID Card New voter registration, corrections, EPIC download via Voter Helpline Apply Online  Online 

🏥 Health Services
→ ABHA Health ID (Ayushman Bharat) Create your Ayushman Bharat Health Account — a unique digital health ID linking all your medical records Apply Online  Online 
→ PM-JAY Beneficiary Check Check if your family is eligible for ₹5 lakh health cover under PM Jan Arogya Yojana Check Status  Online 
→ CoWIN Vaccination Certificate Download COVID-19 vaccination certificate; correct name or date; international travel certificate Download  Online 

🚗 Transport Services
→ Driving Licence Learner licence, permanent DL, renewal, address change, international driving permit Apply Online  Online 
→ Vehicle Registration New registration, transfer of ownership, RC renewal, hypothecation Apply Online  Online 
→ FASTag Registration Get a FASTag for toll payments; link to vehicle; recharge and track transactions Apply Online  Online 

🎓 Education Services
→ National Scholarship Portal Apply for central and state scholarships for SC/ST/OBC/Minority students; track application Apply Online  Online 
→ DigiLocker Academic Docs Access digitally signed mark sheets, degrees, and certificates from CBSE, universities, and boards Access Now  Online
