Grievance Process

① Submit grievance online — receive registration number via SMS within 2 hours.
② Grievance forwarded to concerned department within 24 hours.
③ Department responds within 30 working days. You can track status online.
④ If unsatisfied, escalate to senior authority or file RTI.


Escalation options:
 ·  CPGRAMS (Central Public Grievance)
 ·  Right to Information (RTI) Portal
 ·  Lokpal / Lokayukta
 ·  PMO Grievance Portal
