Public Welfare Schemes
Government programmes designed to improve lives across health, agriculture, housing, and more.

🏥 Health & Social Security
PM-JAY
Ayushman Bharat
Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana

Provides health coverage of ₹5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary hospital care. Covers over 50 crore beneficiaries — the world's largest government-funded health insurance scheme.

Eligibility: Based on SECC 2011 data; BPL families
Benefit: Cashless treatment at empanelled hospitals

Check Eligibility | Enroll Now 		NHM
National Health Mission
Reproductive, Maternal, Newborn, Child Health

Provides free maternal and child health services including antenatal care, institutional delivery, immunisation, and nutrition services through government health centres.

Eligibility: All pregnant women, infants, and children
Benefit: Free OPD, medicines, diagnostic services

🌾 Agriculture & Farmer Welfare
INCOME SUPPORT
PM-KISAN

Direct income support of ₹6,000 per year in three instalments of ₹2,000 to all land-holding farmer families across the country.

Who qualifies: Land-owning farmer families
Exclusions: Income tax payers, govt employees

Register / Check Status 		CROP INSURANCE
PM Fasal Bima Yojana

Financial support to farmers whose crops are damaged due to unforeseen calamities, pests, or diseases. Premium rates as low as 1.5% for Rabi crops.

Who qualifies: All farmers growing notified crops
Enrolment: Via bank or CSC before season cutoff

PM Krishi Sinchai Yojana

"Har Khet Ko Pani, More Crop Per Drop" — expands irrigation coverage and promotes water use efficiency through micro-irrigation subsidies.

Who qualifies: Farmers with land records
Subsidy: Up to 55% on drip/sprinkler systems

🏠 Housing & Urban Development
HOUSING
PM Awas Yojana – Urban

Provides affordable housing to urban poor through interest subsidies on home loans and direct beneficiary transfer for house construction.

Interest subsidy: Up to 6.5% for EWS/LIG categories
Eligibility: No pucca house owned in India; income limits apply

Check Eligibility | Apply 		HOUSING
PM Awas Yojana – Gramin

Financial assistance of ₹1.20 lakh (plains) to ₹1.30 lakh (hills/NE) per unit to rural BPL households for construction of pucca houses with basic amenities.

Eligibility: Rural BPL households per SECC data
Includes: Toilet, LPG connection, electricity

⚡ Energy & Employment
LPG
PM Ujjwala Yojana

Free LPG connections to women from BPL households to replace harmful solid fuel cooking, improving health and reducing indoor air pollution.

Benefit: Free connection + first refill + stove

Apply / Know More 		ELECTRICITY
Saubhagya Scheme

Free electricity connections to all un-electrified households in rural areas and economically poor urban households to achieve universal electrification.

Benefit: Free connection + LED bulbs + switch

MGNREGS

Guarantees 100 days of wage employment per year to every rural household whose adult members are willing to do unskilled manual work.

Wage: State-notified minimum wage
