OUR STORY

We got tired of tools
that made us work harder.

NovaMind started in a shared apartment in Bengaluru in 2024. We were three engineers and a designer, building our own startup — and drowning in tools. Jira for tasks, Notion for docs, Slack for everything else, and still missing what mattered. The information existed. The intelligence didn't.

We spent a year building the AI layer we wished existed: something that reads across your entire stack, learns how your team actually works, and acts as a quiet co-pilot that anticipates rather than reacts. Today, NovaMind is trusted by 12,000 teams in 40 countries.

Our mission is simple: make every person on your team feel like they have a brilliant colleague who has read everything, forgotten nothing, and always has five minutes for you.
