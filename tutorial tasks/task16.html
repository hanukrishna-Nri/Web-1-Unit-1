Audio Example

The audio tag is used to add sound or music to a webpage.



Audio Tag Attributes:

  • controls – Shows play/pause/volume buttons
  • autoplay – Plays audio automatically when page loads
  • loop – Repeats the audio continuously
  • muted – Starts the audio muted

Video Example

The video tag is used to embed a video directly on the webpage.



Video Tag Attributes:

  • controls – Shows video player buttons
  • autoplay – Video starts playing when page opens
  • loop – Keeps repeating the video
  • muted – Video plays without sound
  • poster – Image shown before the video plays
